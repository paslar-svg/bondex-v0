Economic Model; Demand Forces

Three structural forces create sustained token demand beyond speculation:

Operational Spend; enterprises pay BDXN for job listings, ATS access, and premium placement. Recurring budget-line spend.

Lockups; staking for reputation tiers, governance participation, and revenue-share eligibility removes circulating supply.

Trust Collateral; both sides of the market lock BDXN to signal credibility. The token exists because trust needs collateral.

Bond Points

Off-chain reputation score. Earned through profile completeness, verifications, referrals, and professional contributions. Non-transferable. Measures behavior.

BDXN Token

On-chain economic asset. Used for hiring fees, staking, governance, premium access, and trust collateral. Transferable and tradeable. Captures value.
